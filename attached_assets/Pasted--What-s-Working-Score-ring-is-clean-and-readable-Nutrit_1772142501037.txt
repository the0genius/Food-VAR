🔴 Critical Issues

The white card around it has too much dead space. The ring itself looks thin and lightweight — not premium. The glow effect isn't visible at all.
2. Product name area is completely unstyled
"Oreo Original" and "Oreo" below it look like default React Native text — no visual hierarchy, no breathing room, no card container. It looks unfinished compared to the rest.
3. Highlight bullets are weak
Sugar, Carbs, Fiber rows (image 2) are just plain text with a red dot. They look like a basic list, not a premium health app. No visual weight.
4. "Searched" chip is unstyled
The access method chip "Searched" has no color, no background — looks broken next to the green "Snacks" chip.
5. Score ring glow is missing
The spec called for a subtle glow behind the arc. Not visible at all — the ring just floats on a plain white card.
6. Scan Another button (image 3)
Full solid green is too heavy. Should be a gradient button matching the rest of the app.
7. Allergen section (image 3)
The allergen chips (Gluten, Soy) look like error tags from a form — not premium. Should be more visually alarming given the severity.
8. No visual separation between sections
Everything bleeds into each other — score ring → product info → verdict → nutrition. No breathing room or clear section boundaries.
